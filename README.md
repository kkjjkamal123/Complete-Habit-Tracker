## Features

- **Dashboard** — your day at a glance: stat cards, sparklines, a week/month chart, up-next tasks
- **To-Do** — priorities and due dates; completed tasks fade and sink
- **Time** — a live, persistent stopwatch plus manual logging
- **Habits** — streaks with a rolling 7-day history
- **Goals** — monthly targets with animated progress
- **Review** — an end-of-day mood check-in and reflection journal
- **Sync** — optional cross-device sync through your own Firebase

Plus an accent-colour picker, light & dark themes, and animation controls that keep it smooth on any device.

## Local-first & private

Your data lives on your device and is the source of truth — no account needed. Connect your own
free Firebase from the in-app **Sync** tab to keep phone ⇄ desktop ⇄ web in sync through *your*
database. Turn it off anytime; everything keeps working offline.

## Built with

React 19 · TypeScript · Vite · Framer Motion · Capacitor (Android) · Electron (desktop) · Firebase (optional)
